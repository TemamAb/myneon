# User Journeys: Executive Owner vs. Client

## 1. The Journey of the Executive Owner
**Role:** The Architect / Builder of the System.
**Interface:** Platform Mode (ExecutiveDashboard)
**Goal:** To forge a bespoke financial instrument (the "Flash Loan Bot") and hand it over to a client or deploy it for themselves.

### Step 1: Initialization & Analysis
*   The Executive logs into the **AION Platform**.
*   They see the **Cluster Unit Dissection**, a high-tech grid of all available AI modules (Risk engine, Arbitrage scanner, MEV shield).
*   They run a **Deep Scan** to identify any logical gaps (Simulated "Audit").

### Step 2: Fabrication (The "Forge")
*   The Executive customizes the identity of the bot (e.g., naming it "QUANTUM ARB V9").
*   They initiate the **One Click Sovereign Run**.
*   **The System Reacts**:
    1.  Compiles the `AineonFlashLoan.sol` contract.
    2.  Simulates the deployment to Ethereum Mainnet.
    3.  Links Oracle feeds (Chainlink/API3).
    4.  Generates a **Sovereign DNA Sheet**, a cryptographic receipt of the build.

### Step 3: Delivery
*   The Executive reviews the final DNA Manifest.
*   They click **"Receive DNA Manifest & Run"**.
*   This action **transfers control** to the Client Interface (Trader Mode).

## 2. The Journey of the Client
**Role:** The Operator / Trader.
**Interface:** Trader Mode (ClientDashboard)
**Goal:** To safely test the system and then realize profit using the instrument built by the Executive.

### Step 1: Reception & Safe Mode (Simulation)
*   The Client lands on a clean, minimalist **Trader Dashboard**.
*   **Default State**: `SIMULATION MODE` (Blue/Green Theme).
*   **Experience**: The client watches the bot identify potential trades. The "Projected Yield" ticker moves up and down based on market volatility logic.
*   **Safety**: No real funds are at risk. They see "SAFE MODE ACTIVE" in the footer. This builds trust in the logic.

### Step 2: The Decision (The "Toggle")
*   Once confident, the Client clicks the **"Sim / Live"** toggle in the header.
*   The interface shifts dramatically:
    *   **Color Shift**: From soothing Blue to alert Amber/Gold.
    *   **Status**: "SIMULATION MESH" becomes "MAINNET LIQUIDITY UPLINK".
    *   **Data**: Infinite scrolling fake data is replaced by *real* block times and gas prices (simulated connection for now).

### Step 3: Capital Realization (Live Mode)
*   The system is now "Capital at Risk".
*   The "Realized Yield" ticker now represents ostensibly real value flowing through the custom `AineonFlashLoan.sol` contract.
*   The Client monitors the "Wealth Pulse", knowing this is the live execution of the text they purchased.

## 3. The "Economical Mode" Underpinning
To make this journey viable without burning ETH on every test:
*   **Simulation Mode**: Runs locally in the browser (React state). Zero Cost.
*   **Live Mode**: Hooks into a **Local Hardhat Fork**. It *looks* and *feels* exactly like Mainnet (same block numbers, same Uniswap pools), but uses "Simulated Mainnet ETH".
*   **Benefit**: The Client gets the full visceral experience of "Live Trading" without the Executive paying gas fees during the demo phase.
